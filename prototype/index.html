Need to see more reasons to get excited about camping with family? Check this out:

Geocaching
Geocaching is always such a fun thing for kids and adults alike to do! It is like a treasure hunt, sometimes there is only a thing you sign and sometimes there is little goodies!
Club 52 Passport Program
They give out free little Passport books at each Visitors center, and you can collect stamps for free at each park. You get prizes for certain milestones, such as free cards and a free shirt!
Backpacking
I have never been on a backpacking trip yet...but I am working up to it!
2024 Eclipse
They always do such cool things during eclipses!

Hopefully this will get you excited and want to go outdoors with your family as well.
