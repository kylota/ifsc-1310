Progressive Enhancement Writing Assignment

The main difference between the original mindset of design - graceful degradation - and progressive enhancement is foresight. When sites were first being designed, everything was new and we were not sure if it would even last as a commonly used tool, let alone get as big as it did. Therefore, no one really knew how to prepare for the future. As web design begin picking up speed and it became clear that it was here to stay, designers began to realize that no matter how awesome a language may seem at this time, there would always be another that came after it.
